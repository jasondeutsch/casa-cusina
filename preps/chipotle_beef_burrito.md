#  Chipotle Beef Burrito 


## Nutrition

Servings: 5

per serving...

|     |    |
|-------------|-------------|
Calories | 600
Protein  | 46g

## Ingredients

### Main
- 5 Burrito sized tortillas
- 2 lbs 93/7 ground beef
- 0.5 cup chicken stock

### Sauce

- 1 small can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
- 5 TBS Kewpie Mayo
- 0.5 TBS chili powder
- .5 tbs sour cream
- 1 tbs salt
- 0.5 tbs black pepper
- 1 TBS garlic powder
- 1 TBS onion powder
- 1 TBS cumin
- 1 TSP msg
